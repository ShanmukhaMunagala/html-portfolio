The Best Movies According to Shanmukha

My top 3 movies of all time

RRR

This is first Telugu movie awarded Oscar.
And acting of Jr.NTR and Ramcharan was Awesome!
This movie was directed by SS Rajamouli.

Maharshi

Maharshi was the film directed by Vamshi Paidipally.
Mahesh Babu, Allari Naresh and Pooja Hegde acted as main roles.
This movie is based upon Success and Friendship.

Adhipurush

Adhipurush was the film directed by Om Raut.
This movie was based on the Epic "Ramayan" but In this they have been taken only reference of Ramayan.
Songs and BGM's are the main positive of this film.
